Emperor Sheev Palpatine: The Senate
Emperor Palpatine taking a break from ruling the Galaxy.

Emperor Palpatine is the Senate, Emperor and a Dark Lord of the Sith. He defeated the Jedi and took control of the Galactic Republic in a long scheme for control. After taking over he controlled the galaxy for decades with the help of his apprentice, Darth Vader. Palpatine was finally defeated during the Battle of Endor, where Vader turned on him to save his son, Luke Skywalker.
